Grape leaves disease and treatment

Anthracnose, brown spot, mites, black rot, downy mildew, and leaf blight are six common grape leaf pests and diseases, which cause severe economic losses to the grape industry.

Spray a sulfur fungicide, such as wettable sulfur, on the grape plants during their wooly bud stage in late winter. A sulfur fungicide will help get rid of rust fungus and slows down rust mites.
